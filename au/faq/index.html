Frequently Asked Questions

Simple answers to all your questions.

What is Nimbo X?

A cloud application that helps physicians and medical organizations have a better control of their patient’s data and operations. Nimbo X integrates a practice management system with an electronic health record so that you can have an easy control of your whole operation from a single easy to use app.

How much does Nimbo X cost?

The most basic version of Nimbo X is free. The full version of Nimbo X has a low monthly subscription fee. You can see our pricing page to find the Nimbo X plan that suits your organization’s needs.

How can I start using Nimbo X?

You can create your free account here! It’s as easy as signing up for an email or facebook account. Since Nimbo X is in the cloud, you don't have to download or install any software.

Who can access my patients information?

Only people who you have invited to be part of your organization on Nimbo X will have access to the patient's data. Assistant accounts will only have access to patients demographic information and to the schedule, physicians can access any patient's record as they need.

Where is my patient's data stored?

All of your organization’s data is stored in our server in the United States. The security and privacy of your clinical data is our number one priority, that’s why we follow the highest standards of security and encryption.

What do I need to be able to use Nimbo X?

You just need to have an internet connection on any devices that has an internet browser; weather it be a laptop, tablet or smartphone you can access Nimbo X from anywhere.

What happens to my information if i stop using Nimbo X?

If you stop using Nimbo X for a period of time, you can always come back to the app and find all of your patients data. If at any moment you want to delete your Nimbo X account you can export all of your patients data.
